# Safety And Trust

HabeshaGo uses local trust features as first-class platform infrastructure:

- Sened verification for merchants, drivers, addresses, landmarks, and community verifiers.
- Address confidence scoring for hard-to-find locations.
- Almaz women-owned merchant verification and discovery filters.
- Lijoch family delivery with PIN and guardian notifications.
- Mesewa night safety with trusted drivers, emergency contacts, and support escalation.
- Gasha senior accounts with priority support and trusted driver preference.

Child delivery should avoid unnecessary storage of children photos. Prefer verification codes, guardian notifications, GPS confirmation, and school or safe-location checks.
