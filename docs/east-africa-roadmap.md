# East Africa Roadmap

## Phase 1: Ethiopia, Bole

- Launch food and grocery delivery in Bole.
- Onboard 5-20 verified merchants.
- Validate cash reconciliation, driver float, support, and order tracking.
- Test offline/SMS fallback and Tenagn voice-command flows in controlled pilots.

## Phase 2: Addis Ababa And Ethiopia

- Expand to all Addis Ababa sub-cities.
- Add Adama, Bahir Dar, Hawassa, Dire Dawa, Mekelle, and Gondar.
- Add pharmacy, corporate lunch, home services, and Kelemat marketplace pilots.

## Phase 3: East Africa

- Kenya: KES, Swahili, English, M-Pesa, Airtel Money.
- Tanzania: TZS, Swahili, English, M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa.
- Uganda: UGX, English, Luganda, Swahili, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money.
- Rwanda: RWF, Kinyarwanda, English, French, MoMo, Airtel Money.
- South Sudan: SSP, English, Arabic, Juba Arabic, MTN MoMo, Zain Cash.
